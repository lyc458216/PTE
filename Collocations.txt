第一类：动词 + 介词 (Verb + Preposition)

account for - 解释；占据（比例）
Electronic goods account for 30% of our exports. (电子产品占我们要出口额的 30%。)
adhere to - 遵守；坚持
All staff must adhere to the safety regulations. (所有员工必须遵守安全规定。)
agree with - 同意
The results do not agree with the earlier findings. (这些结果与早期的发现不一致。)
aim at - 针对；瞄准
The new policy is aimed at reducing poverty. (新政策旨在减少贫困。)
allow for - 考虑到；顾及
We must allow for possible delays in the schedule. (我们必须考虑到日程安排中可能出现的延误。)
amount to - 总计；相当于
The bill amounted to over $500. (账单总计超过 500 美元。)
apologize for - 为...道歉
The airline apologized for the cancellation. (航空公司为取消航班道歉。)
appeal to - 呼吁；吸引
The design aims to appeal to younger consumers. (该设计旨在吸引年轻消费者。)
apply to - 适用于；申请
This rule does not apply to international students. (这条规则不适用于国际学生。)
associate with - 与...有联系
Pollution is closely associated with industrial growth. (污染与工业增长密切相关。)
attribute to - 归因于
Scientists attribute the warming to greenhouse gases. (科学家将变暖归因于温室气体。)
base on - 基于
Conclusions should be based on solid evidence. (结论应基于确凿的证据。)
believe in - 相信
He strongly believes in the value of education. (他坚信教育的价值。)
benefit from - 从...中获益
Small businesses will benefit from the tax cuts. (小企业将从减税中受益。)
blame for - 因...责怪
The pilot was blamed for the accident. (飞行员因事故受到指责。)

Nurses care for patients in the hospital. (护士在医院照顾病人。)
cater to - 迎合（需求）
The hotel caters to business travelers. (这家酒店迎合商务旅客的需求。)
collaborate with - 与...合作
The university will collaborate with local industries. (大学将与当地产业合作。)
comment on - 评论
The minister refused to comment on the scandal. (部长拒绝评论这起丑闻。)
compare with/to - 与...比较
The output cannot compare with last year’s figures. (产量无法与去年的数据相比。)
compete with - 与...竞争
Small shops struggle to compete with large supermarkets. (小商店难以与大型超市竞争。)
comply with - 遵守（规定）
The building must comply with fire safety standards. (建筑物必须遵守消防安全标准。)
concentrate on - 集中精力于
You should concentrate on your studies. (你应该专注于学业。)
conclude with - 以...结束
The lecture concluded with a Q&A session. (讲座以问答环节结束。)
confuse with - 与...混淆
Do not confuse confidence with arrogance. (不要把自信和傲慢混为一谈。)
consist of - 由...组成
The team consists of five members. (该团队由五名成员组成。)
contribute to - 有助于；促成；捐献
Smoking can contribute to lung cancer. (吸烟会导致肺癌。)
cope with - 处理；应对
It is difficult to cope with stress. (应对压力很困难。)
correspond to - 与...一致；对应
The numbers correspond to points on the map. (这些数字对应地图上的点。)
count on - 依靠；指望
We can count on his support. (我们可以指望他的支持。)
deal with - 处理
The government must deal with the economic crisis. (政府必须处理经济危机。)
dedicate to - 致力于
She dedicated her life to science. (她把一生献给了科学。)
depend on/upon - 取决于；依赖
Success depends on hard work. (成功取决于努力工作。)
derive from - 源于
Many English words derive from Latin. (许多英语单词源于拉丁语。)
differ from - 不同于
My opinion differs from yours. (我的观点与你的不同。)
dispose of - 处理；丢弃
Nuclear waste is hard to dispose of. (核废料很难处理。)
distinguish from - 区别于
It is hard to distinguish fact from fiction. (很难区分事实与虚构。)
divide into - 分成

engage in - 参与；从事

equip with - 配备

exclude from - 排除在外
Women were excluded from the club. (女性被排除在该俱乐部之外。)
expose to - 暴露于
Do not expose the skin to direct sunlight. (不要让皮肤暴露在阳光直射下。)
focus on - 专注于
The research focuses on renewable energy. (这项研究专注于可再生能源。)
impose on - 强加于
New taxes were imposed on luxury goods. (奢侈品被征收了新税。)
insist on - 坚持
They insisted on a refund. (他们坚持要求退款。)
interact with - 与...互动
Children learn by interacting with others. (儿童通过与他人互动来学习。)
interfere with - 干扰；妨碍

invest in - 投资于
The company plans to invest in new technology. (公司计划投资新技术。)
involve in - 卷入；参与
He was involved in a car accident. (他卷入了一场车祸。)
lead to - 导致
Poor diet can lead to health problems. (不良饮食会导致健康问题。)
link to - 连接到；与...有关
Stress is linked to heart disease. (压力与心脏病有关。)
object to - 反对
Residents objected to the new airport plan. (居民反对新机场计划。)
participate in - 参加
Everyone is encouraged to participate in the event. (鼓励大家参与该活动。)
prevent from - 阻止
The rain prevented us from playing. (雨阻止了我们游玩。)
prohibit from - 禁止
Staff are prohibited from smoking inside. (严禁员工在室内吸烟。)
protect from - 保护免受...
Sunscreen protects the skin from UV rays. (防晒霜保护皮肤免受紫外线伤害。)
provide with - 提供
The school provides students with books. (学校为学生提供书籍。)
react to - 对...做出反应
The market reacted negatively to the news. (市场对该消息反应消极。)
refer to - 参考；指的是
Please refer to the user manual. (请参考用户手册。)
rely on - 依赖
Many people rely on public transport. (许多人依赖公共交通。)
respond to - 回应
The body responds to infection by producing fever. (身体通过发烧来应对感染。)
result in - 导致（结果）
The talks resulted in a peace treaty. (会谈达成了一项和平条约。)
result from - 起因于
The damage resulted from the storm. (损害是由暴风雨造成的。)
search for - 寻找
Scientists are searching for a cure. (科学家正在寻找治愈方法。)
specialize in - 专攻
The lawyer specializes in contract law. (这位律师专攻合同法。)
stem from - 源于
His problems stem from a lack of confidence. (他的问题源于缺乏自信。)
subscribe to - 订阅；同意（观点）
Many people subscribe to the view that education is key. (许多人认同教育是关键这一观点。)
succeed in - 成功做某事
He finally succeeded in solving the puzzle. (他终于成功解开了谜题。)
suffer from - 遭受；患病
The patient suffers from asthma. (该患者患有哮喘。)
translate into - 翻译成；转化为
Ideas must be translated into action. (想法必须转化为行动。)
vote for - 投票支持
Citizens will vote for a new president. (公民将投票选出新总统。)
wait for - 等待
We are waiting for the results. (我们在等待结果。)
