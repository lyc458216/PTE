四段式结构:
1.引题立论.提出现象+ 给出你的明确选择.
2.正方论证:提出你的核心优点1 +用个人经历详细证明.
3.让步反驳:承认对方优点+ 转折反驳+ 提出你的核心优点2.
4.总结重申:总结原因+ 重申你的选择.

第一段:重述背景与题目+表明观点
The controversial topic with respect to____ never fails to attract public attention.(背景)
There has been a heated debate on whether ____.(题目)
As far as I am concerned, I tend to be the advocate/opponent regarding this issue. 

第二段:观点1论述与解释
It is evidently reasonable for me to believe that this is good(观点1)
This is because ____.(解释)
To illustrate this, my personal experience is a convincing example. 
As a result,  ____. (总结)

第三段:让步反驳段(反方观点+观点2/反驳)
In contrast, it is commonly believed as a fact that ____(反方观点)
Nevertheless, this argument does not hold water. 
It should be pointed out that ____(观点2/反驳)
That is to say, ____.(解释2)

第四段:总结段落
In a nutshell, due to the mentioned reasons, we may safely reach the conclusion that ____.(立场)
