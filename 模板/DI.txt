PTE Describe Image (DI) 万能高分模板

为什么你需要 DI 模板？
在 PTE 口语考试中，Describe Image (DI) 是一项对流利度要求极高的题型。机器评分的核心逻辑在于：流利度 > 发音 > 内容。

很多考生在看到复杂的图表时容易卡壳，导致流利度分数大跌。使用一套熟练的模板，可以帮助你在 25 秒的准备时间内快速组织语言，并在 40 秒的答题时间内保持 5.0 语速不卡顿。

DI 万能高分模板
这套模板适用于 90% 以上的图表题（线图、柱图、饼图、地图、流程图等）。

模板结构
Introduction (开头)
The image gives information about [Title].
Body (主体 - 找最大、最小、趋势)
From the image, I can see [Item A] is the highest figure, which is about [Number].
From the image, I can also see [Item B] is the lowest figure, which is about [Number].
Also, I can see the trend is changing quickly.
Conclusion (结尾)
In conclusion, the image is very informative.

进阶版模板 (针对高分段)
如果你追求 79+ 甚至满分，可以在主体部分增加更多细节，但切记不要牺牲流利度。
开头: 
The graph provided reveals data regarding [Title].
主体:
According to the graph, it is clear that [Maximum Value] requires the most attention.
On the contrary, [Minimum Value] is the least significant one.
Moreover, figures for [Category] are increasing/decreasing significantly.
结尾: Overall, this chart effectively illustrates the variations in [Topic].

实战演示
假设通过一道柱状图，标题是 "Population of Major Cities"。
你的回答应该是：
"The image gives information about Population of Major Cities. 
It is a beautiful image. 
From the image, I can see Tokyo is the highest figure, which is about 37 million. 
From the image, I can also see New York is the lowest figure, which is about 18 million. 
Also, I can see the trend is changing quickly. 
In conclusion, the image is very informative."

备考小贴士
不要纠结数据准确性：机器不核对你读的数字是否精确，只听你读得顺不顺。看到 "19,873" 读不出来？直接读 "about 20 thousand" 或者单纯读 "number 20"。
遇到生词怎么办：直接用 "something" 代替，或者随便读一个你认识的词，绝对不要卡顿。
保持匀速：不要忽快忽慢，全程保持平稳的语调和语速。
无脑套用：遇到地图题、流程图甚至无标题图，只要能抓到几个关键词，就往模板里套。
