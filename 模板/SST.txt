引导（direct）其他蜜蜂到花蜜的源头（source of nectar）
实验研究：
觅食的蜜蜂（foraging bees）被引入了危险（dangers），所以它们回到蜂巢（hive）去阻止（stop）跳舞的蜜蜂
结论：
头部撞击（headbutt）是一种警告信号（warning signal），这种行为是警告跳舞的蜜蜂（dancing bees）花蜜的危险。
答案：
Honey bees do a waggle dance, direct other bees to the source of nectar. 
According to the research, 
foraging bees(觅食的蜜蜂) were introduced to the dangers in the experiment(在实验中), 
so they returned to hive to stop the dancing bees. 
In conclusion, we can know that a headbutt is a warning signal, 
and the behavior warns dancing bees about the dangers from the nectar.
